Welcome to Cerv.

I wrote this program as I feel sometimes websites can be a letdown. They can often go down leaving you stranded. I figured a desktop application would be a perfect substitute for this problem, its much faster also. As long as you have any form of PC and an internet connection which are the basic requirements of the program, it will work. P.s you need Java 8+ also.
